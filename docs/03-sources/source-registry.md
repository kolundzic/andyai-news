# andyai.news — Source Registry

**Status:** locked  
**Version:** v0.1  
**Scope:** pilot  
**Owner:** Andy + AI  
**Purpose:** Defines trusted source classes, trust levels, and source usage rules for andyai.news.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

## Trust hierarchy

### T1 — official / primary
- official company blogs
- official docs
- official repos / changelogs
- conference pages
- research papers

### T2 — high-quality reporting
- Reuters
- AP
- Financial Times
- Bloomberg
- Ars Technica
- MIT Technology Review
- high-quality tech/business reporting

### T3 — useful secondary insight
- expert blogs
- quality newsletters
- specialist commentary

### T4 — signal only
- social posts
- early ecosystem signals

### T5 — avoid / restricted
- anonymous rumor accounts
- clickbait sites
- weak aggregation chains
- AI spam content farms

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

## Canonical rule

**T1 and T2 form the backbone.  
T3 helps.  
T4 alerts.  
T5 does not lead the editorial process.**
