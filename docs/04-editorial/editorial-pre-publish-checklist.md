# andyai.news — Editorial Pre-Publish Checklist

**Status:** active  
**Version:** v0.1  
**Scope:** pilot  
**Owner:** Andy + AI  
**Purpose:** Practical go/no-go editorial checklist before any article goes live.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

## Pre-publish questions

1. Are the key claims source-grounded?
2. Is it clear what happened?
3. Is it clear why it matters?
4. Does the article fit the site and the category?
5. Does the headline inform without clickbait?
6. Does the intro explain the topic quickly?
7. Does the article flow logically?
8. Is there enough context?
9. Are facts and interpretation clearly separated?
10. Is uncertainty honestly marked?
11. Does the tone feel calm and professional?
12. Is there any fluff to cut?
13. Does the reader get real value?
14. Would we stand behind this publicly?

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

## Canonical rule

**If two or more core answers feel weak, the article is not publish-ready.**
