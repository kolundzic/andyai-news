**Status:** active  
**Version:** v0.1  
**Scope:** pilot  
**Owner:** Andy + AI  
**Purpose:** Final readiness framework for judging the pilot as NOT READY, SOFT READY, or READY.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

## Readiness states

### NOT READY
Critical pilot gaps still block a healthy public-facing stance.

### SOFT READY
Stable enough for disciplined public operation while still improving.

### READY
Strong enough to stand as a clear proof-point.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

## P0 launch gates

- homepage clarity
- article readability
- archive logic
- taxonomy discipline
- source trust
- editorial consistency
- publishing routine exists
- pre-publish review exists
- mobile usability is good
- deployment is safe enough

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

## Canonical rule

**We do not chase perfection.  
We chase a stable, honest, professional pilot.  
First SOFT READY. Then READY.**
